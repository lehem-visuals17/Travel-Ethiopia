About This Experience

Explore the vibrant street food scene and discover authentic local flavors. Taste tibs, sambusas, and traditional dishes while learning about Ethiopian culinary culture.

What's Included

  • Local food guide
  • 10+ food tastings
  • Soft drinks
  • Cultural insights

Not Included

  • Hotel pickup and drop-off
  • Alcoholic beverages
  • Gratuities

Itinerary

1

Meet at Piazza District 11:00 AM

Meet your local guide at the historic Piazza area and receive a brief introduction to Ethiopian street food culture.

2

First Food Stop - Sambusas 11:15 AM

Try freshly made sambusas (samosas) filled with lentils or meat at a popular local stall.

3

Spice Market Visit 11:45 AM

Walk through the aromatic spice market and learn about traditional Ethiopian spices like berbere and mitmita.

4

Tibs and Kitfo Tasting 12:15 PM

Sample authentic tibs (sautéed meat) and kitfo (minced raw beef) at a renowned local eatery.

5

Coffee and Dessert 12:45 PM

End with traditional Ethiopian coffee and sweet treats at a neighborhood café.

6

Tour Conclusion 1:30 PM

Receive recommendations for additional dining spots and say goodbye to your guide.
